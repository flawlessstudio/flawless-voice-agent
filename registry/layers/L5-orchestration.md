# L5 — Orchestration

## Core: Vapi + Retell AI

**Why**: Vapi for rapid iteration, flow flexibility and developer experience. Retell for phone automation depth and production-grade call management. Combined they cover the full orchestration surface.

**Docs**: https://docs.vapi.ai | https://docs.retellai.com

## Watchlist

| Candidate | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| LiveKit Agents | 8 | Open-source, WebRTC-native. Strong for custom architectures. |
| Bland AI | 7 | Simple outbound. Less flexible for complex flows. |
| Custom orchestration | 7 | Full control. High maintenance cost. |

## Criteria for promotion
- Must support multi-path routing (fast/deep/fallback).
- Must support human handoff with context transfer.
- Must support stateless operation for horizontal scaling.
