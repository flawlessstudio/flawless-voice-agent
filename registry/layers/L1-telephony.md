# L1 — Telephony

## Core: Twilio

**Why**: Maximum maturity, programmable voice, SIP trunking, outbound routing, compliance controls and the largest ecosystem for production voice at scale. Handles 10k+ concurrent calls with subcaccount pools and configurable call limits.

**Docs**: https://www.twilio.com/docs/voice

| Vonage | 7 | Competitive pricing, good SIP. Less ecosystem. |
| Bandwidth | 7 | US carrier-grade. Strong compliance. Less programmable. |

## Criteria for promotion
- Must demonstrate 10k+ concurrent call stability.
- Must have programmable voice API with webhook support.
- Must support TCPA/DNC compliance controls.
