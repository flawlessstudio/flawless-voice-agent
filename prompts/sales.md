# Sales Prompt

## Goal
Qualify the prospect and move them to the next stage of the pipeline.

## Key questions
1. Are you currently using [solution category]?
2. What is your biggest challenge with [pain area]?
3. Do you have a budget allocated for this?
4. Who is involved in the decision?
5. What is your timeline?

## Qualification criteria (BANT)
- Budget: confirmed
- Authority: decision maker or influencer
- Need: pain confirmed
- Timeline: within 90 days
